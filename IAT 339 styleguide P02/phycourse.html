Class Detail

physics: kinematics and dynamics

An introduction to classical mechanics with topics focuses on motion of the macroscopic world including Newton's Laws of Motions, Projectile motion, transitional and rotational motion, momentum, consesrvation ofnenergy and circular motions.

Note: Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, classes will be delivered online until further notice.

topics includes

I: System particles and Mechanisms

Introduces basic properties to describe motion such as position, displacement, distance, velocity and acceleration.

II: Motion of Rigid Bodies

Rotation of Rigid bodies, centripetal and corioolis acceleration.

III: Lagrange Dynamics

Hamilton’s principle; Lagrange’s equations; principle of virtual work. Application of Lagrange’s equation problems of rigid body dynamics.

course includes

  • 20 hour lecture
  • online exercise
  • additional tutoring with flexible hours
  • 1 year access class material
